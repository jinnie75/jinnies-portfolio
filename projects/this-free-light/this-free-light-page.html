    Project Description

    A tactical flashlight that only emits light after a number of advertisements are played. Tactical flashlights are very strong flashlights that are often used for self-defense or law enforcement purposes.

    In a world where our attention is commoditised and traded for money, where do we draw the line?

    This piece was featured in the [Ars Machina · Corpus Digitum] exhibition, held at the South River Art Studios (Georgia, Atlanta) from 10/17 to 10/26, 2025.

    Materials

    parts from a tactical flashlight, ESP 32, audio amplifier circuit, speaker, 3D printed plastic, powerbank
