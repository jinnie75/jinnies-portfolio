    • Find a tree, carve a hole (2025)

    Project Description

    Inspired by a scene in 'In the Mood for Love (2000)' where the protagonist carves a hole in a tree to whisper a secret into and seals it with mud, this is a recreation of that experience in a furniture-like adaptation. When the audience turns the handles on each side, the box opens up a hole where the secret can be kept. After the confession, one can turn back the handle to seal the box closed.

    This piece was featured in the [Lab from New York] exhibition, held at the National Science Museum (Daejeon, South Korea) from 1/6 to 1/11, 2026.

    Materials

    wood, 3D-printed plastic, stainless steel, LED

    Technology

    mechanism design, rapid prototyping using cardboard and 3D printing, exhibition design
